# Support

form4lab is a maintained, solo side project — it is **not a supported product**. There is no SLA, no guaranteed response time, and no support channel or chat.

## What's in scope

Bug reports against the data pipeline, backtester/engine, or SEC EDGAR ingestion are welcome **if they include a reproduction**: the command you ran, what you expected, what actually happened, and — where relevant — a minimal fixture or the exact ticker/filing that triggers it. These may get fixed when the maintainer has time; there's no guarantee of when, or that they will be.

Please open an issue using the bug report template rather than emailing directly.

If you'd like to fix something yourself, see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

## What's out of scope

The following will be closed, generally with a pointer to [DISCLAIMER.md](DISCLAIMER.md):

- Investment questions — "should I buy X", "is this a good signal", and similar.
- "Will this make money" or strategy-performance questions.
- Requests to debug, tune, or validate *your own* strategy built on top of form4lab.
- General trading, tax, or legal advice.

form4lab ships pedagogical example strategies and no validated trading edge. Nothing in this repository is investment advice — see [DISCLAIMER.md](DISCLAIMER.md).
