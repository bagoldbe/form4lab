Methodology & Caveats

Temporal Scoring: Each insider's tier and skill score are computed using only data available before the transaction date. No future outcomes leak into classification. This ensures the backtest reflects real-world signal quality.

Capital Constraints: Starting with ${{ "{:,.0f}".format(metrics.initial_cash) }}. Position sizing, margin, and idle-cash parking follow your configured settings — see .env.example. When cash runs out, signals are skipped.

Hold Period: Positions are held for 60 trading days, then sold at market close. No early exits, no stop losses.

Caveats:

  • Universe covers the ticker lists you configure — see form4lab/data/universes. Results on any fixed list carry survivorship bias.
  • No transaction costs, slippage, or bid-ask spreads modeled
  • Backtests spanning sharp crashes and recoveries (e.g. 2020) can inflate returns relative to calmer periods
  • Past performance does not guarantee future results
